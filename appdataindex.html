HabitMood — Data & Account Deletion Instructions

Delete Your Data (without deleting your account)

If you want to remove your habit, mood, and completion history from HabitMood but keep your account active, follow these steps:

  1. Open the HabitMood app
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll to the Data section
  4. Tap Clear all data
  5. Confirm the action when prompted

What gets deleted: All habits, habit completions, and mood entries associated with your account.

What is kept: Your account login (email/password or Google sign-in) remains active, and your app settings (theme, reminder times) are preserved.

Retention: Once you confirm, this data is deleted immediately from your device. If cloud backup is enabled, it is also removed from our servers within 24 hours.

Delete Your Account

If you want to permanently delete your HabitMood account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the HabitMood app
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll to the Data section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Read the warning carefully — this action is permanent and cannot be undone
  6. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What gets deleted:

  • Your account login credentials (email, password, authentication record)
  • All habits, habit completions, and mood entries
  • All app settings and preferences associated with your account

What is kept: Nothing — this is a full account and data deletion.

Retention: Your account and data are deleted immediately upon confirmation. There is no recovery period — once deleted, this data cannot be restored.

Note: If you have an active Pro subscription purchased through Google Play, deleting your HabitMood account does not automatically cancel your subscription. Please manage or cancel your subscription separately through the Google Play Store app under Subscriptions.

Need Help?

If you're unable to access the app to delete your account or data (for example, if you've lost access to your login), contact us at habitmoodprivacy@gmail.com and we will process your request manually within 30 days.
